كيف تختار استوديو تصميم مناسب؟

5 أبريل 2026 5 دقائق قراءة AY. Design

1. A Real Portfolio

Don\'t be dazzled by volume. Look for depth. Are there case studies explaining the thinking behind the work? A studio showing 50 logos without context is fundamentally different from one showing 10 projects with a full breakdown of challenge, strategy, and result.

2. Process Over Output

A serious studio explains its methodology — research to delivery. Ask: how many phases in your identity process? How does it start? When do I see a first draft? If the answer is "we start right away," run. Chaos in process = chaos in result.

3. Questions Before Design

A professional designer asks dozens of questions before drawing a line:

  • Who is your target audience?
  • Who are your competitors?
  • What are the brand\'s core values?
  • What impression should the project leave?
  • What do you NOT want?

An amateur opens Photoshop on day one. A pro asks, listens, and understands first.

4. Revisions Policy

A serious studio has clear terms: a defined number of revision rounds, and a precise definition of what counts as a "revision" vs. a "complete change." No open-ended unlimited revision promises (usually a sign of process insecurity), and no excessive rigidity that restricts the client.

5. Human Chemistry

This criterion is often overlooked but it\'s among the most important. You\'ll work with this studio for weeks or months. If the first meeting feels off, it won\'t get better. Comfort in communication means you\'ll be honest with feedback — and that makes a massive difference in the final result.

The right studio isn\'t the cheapest or most expensive — it\'s the one that understands you before designing for you.

Conclusion

At AY. Design, these five criteria sit at the core of how we work. Every project starts with questions, follows a clear process, and ends with a result both sides are proud of. Reach out for a real conversation about your project.

قرار اختيار مصمم يحدد مصير مشروعك البصري لسنوات. هذه خمسة معايير عملية.

1. البورتفوليو الحقيقي

ابحث عن العمق وحالات الدراسة، لا عدد الأعمال.

2. العملية لا النتيجة فقط

الاستوديو الجاد يشرح منهجيته من البحث إلى التسليم.

3. السؤال قبل التصميم

المحترف يسأل عشرات الأسئلة قبل أن يرسم خطاً.

4. سياسة المراجعات والتعديلات

سياسة واضحة للتعديلات — لا وعود مفتوحة ولا تشدد مبالغ فيه.

5. الكيمياء البشرية

إذا لم ترتح في الاجتماع الأول، لن ترتاح لاحقاً.

الاستوديو المناسب هو الذي يفهمك قبل أن يصمم لك.
