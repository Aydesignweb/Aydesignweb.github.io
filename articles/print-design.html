Credibility & Weight

In the Saudi market specifically, premium print reflects trust and standing. Major corporations still print annual reports and company profiles on luxury paper for a simple reason: because it works. Print says "we\'re here to stay."

Material Selection

From glossy to raw kraft to matte, every material tells a different story:

  • Glossy: Reflects luxury and modernity, ideal for upscale catalogs.
  • Matte: Suggests quiet elegance and simplicity, ideal for minimalist brands.
  • Kraft: Connects to nature and authenticity, ideal for organic products and cafes.

Special Techniques

Small details transform print from paper to art:

  • Gold/Silver Foil: A touch of luxury on logos and headlines.
  • Embossing: 3D texture that conveys craftsmanship.
  • Laser Cutting: Complex shapes and clean edges impossible with traditional cutting.
  • Spot UV: Selective gloss highlighting specific elements.

Vehicle Wraps

A moving advertisement working 24/7 at no recurring cost. We design vehicle wraps that turn your commercial fleet into mobile billboards. A one-time investment that lasts years.

In a purely digital world, paper speaks louder than ever.

Conclusion

At AY. Design, we craft print worth keeping. Because good print doesn\'t end up in the trash — it sits on the desk.
