الذكاء الاصطناعي في التصميم: شريك لا بديل

5 أبريل 2026 8 دقائق قراءة AY. Design

1. Brainstorming at Light Speed

Previously, ideation took days. Today, we use Midjourney to generate 50 different visual directions in an hour. This massive creative expansion helps us explore territory we couldn\'t reach conventionally.

2. Smart Moodboards

Before starting any project, we build a custom moodboard. Using AI, we generate dozens of coherent visuals that reflect the proposed direction, and present them to the client to pick a path before execution. This saves weeks of later revisions.

3. Mass Customization

Large social campaigns sometimes need 30 coherent visual assets. AI lets us produce them at stable quality with a unified visual signature — cutting production time by 60%.

4. Advanced Art Direction

Firefly and Photoshop AI help us:

  • Remove backgrounds with precision
  • Expand canvases (Generative Expand) without quality loss
  • Manipulate images in ways that were impossible before
  • Create custom textures and patterns

5. But Humans Lead

All these capabilities don\'t change one truth: strategic decisions, taste, cultural context, and human emotion — all remain the designer\'s responsibility. AI doesn\'t understand why green carries different weight in Saudi Arabia. It doesn\'t understand subtle irony. It doesn\'t know when to break the rule.

We lead, the machine executes. That\'s the equation. The designer who masters this partnership excels.

Conclusion

At AY. Design, AI is part of our daily toolkit, like Illustrator and Photoshop. But it\'s not a replacement for vision, nor for years of experience. The future belongs to designers who master both.
